The Stranger — 異鄉人

There are 455,000 strangers in Zurich, a criminally overused novel title, and an obscure band on a faraway island that barely shows up on the map. That band, The Strangers from Taiwan, currently has only 7 listeners in its 5th most popular city.

“The Stranger” — 異鄉人 — in its Chinese characters can be seen as two people covering their faces while eating at the same table: a strange image indeed. I thought that, since strangers ought to connect more, we could sometimes eat with our masks off, even if we’re still strangers.

If we, the strangers in Zurich, click on their song “Rising Sun” a bit more, maybe we can make Zurich the 5th most popular city on Earth for this obscure band. A strange connection of strangers—but maybe it makes the world feel just a little less strange.
